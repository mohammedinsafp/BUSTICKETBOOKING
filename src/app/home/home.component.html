About the Bus Ticket Booking App

The Bus Ticket Booking App provides a user-friendly platform to search, select, and book bus tickets for your desired routes. With a wide range of available bus options, you can easily plan your journey and secure your seat.

Key Features:

  • Search for bus schedules and availability
  • Select your starting point and destination
  • Choose your preferred travel date
  • View bus details including departure time, arrival time, bus number, bus name, bus type, total seats, and fare
  • Book your ticket with a simple click
  • Secure and hassle-free payment process
  • Get instant confirmation and ticket details
  • Manage your bookings and view past trips
  • User-friendly interface and responsive design for a seamless experience
